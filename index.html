Match aliens across the galaxy

How to Play

Objective: Score points by matching 3 or more identical aliens in 10 rounds of 60 seconds each.

Game Mechanics

  • Click adjacent aliens to swap them
  • Match 3+ identical aliens horizontally or vertically
  • Cascading matches create combos for bonus points and time
  • Each round gets faster - test your reflexes!
  • You have 3 lives - meet each round's score threshold or lose a life and retry!

Power-Ups

Visit the Cosmic Shop between rounds to spend your score on upgrades. Choose carefully how you spend your valuable score!
